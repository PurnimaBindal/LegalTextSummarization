These two appeals are directed against Orders-in-Revision No: 3/MS(3)COMMR/RGD/06-07 and 4/MS(4)COMMR/RGD/06-07 both dated 11/10/2006 passed by the Commissioner of Central Excise, Raigad. As the issue involved is common, the appeals are taken up together for consideration and disposal.

2. The appellant, M/s. Rieco Industries Ltd., Roha, received technical know-how from M/s. Intensive Filter GmbH & Co. Germany, and M/s. Clean Gas Systems, Inc., USA during the period 01/11/2002 to 30/04/2003. For the technical know-how received they paid royalty to the foreign service-providers. The department was of the view that the services received falls within the category of Management Consultancy Services and the appellant is liable to pay service tax on reverse charge basis for the period 01/11/2002 to 30/04/2003. Accordingly, service tax demand of ` 42,640/- and ` 9,343/- were confirmed against the appellant along with interest and also imposing equivalent amount of penalties. Aggrieved of the said decision, the appellant is before us.

3. The learned counsel submits that the appellant is not liable to pay any service tax on reverse charge basis on the service received from abroad prior to 18/04/2006 as held by the honble High Court of Bombay in the case of Indian National Ship Owners Association vs. Union of India 2009 (14) STR 289. Therefore, the demands are not sustainable and the impugned order, as far as it pertains to the appellant, be set aside.

4. The learned Superintendent (AR) appearing for the Revenue, fairly concedes that the matter is covered by the decision of the honble High Court of Bombay in the case of Indian National Ship Owners Association vs. Union of India, cited supra.

5. After considering the submissions made by both the sides, we agree that the issue involved is squarely covered by the decision of the honble High Court of Bombay in the case of Indian National Ship Owners Association vs. Union of India, cited supra. Accordingly, we allow the appeal with consequential relief, if any, in accordance with law.
