PER COURT :-

1. Leave to add the State as respondent No.3. Addition be carried out forthwith.

2. Learned AGP causes his appearance on behalf of the added respondent.

3. The learned AGP points out that the petitioner has a statutory remedy of filing a second revision, in view of the judicial pronouncement in Gurudassing Nawoosing Panjwani vs. State of Maharashtra and others [2016 (2) SCC 213].

4. Considering the above, this petition is disposed off with liberty to prefer a second revision.
