PER COURT :-

1. Pursuant to the order dated 5.12.2018 passed in these two matters, learned Advocate for the petitioners has taken instructions and submits on instructions today, that the petitioners would approach the State authorities by preferring a second revision in the light of Gurudassing Nawoosing Panjwani vs. State of Maharashtra and others, 2016 (2) SCC 213. 2. In view of this, both these petitions are disposed off as withdrawn with liberty to prefer the second revision.
