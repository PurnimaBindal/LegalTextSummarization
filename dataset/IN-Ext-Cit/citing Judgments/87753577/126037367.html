PER COURT :-

1 Though the learned Advocate for the Petitioners has strenuously canvassed his grounds, I find that the Petitioners have a statutory remedy of approaching the State by filing a second revision under Section 257 of the Maharashtra Land Revenue Code in view of the judgment of the Honourable Supreme Court in the matter of Gurudassing Nawoosing Panjwani vs. State of Maharashtra and others, 2016 (2) SCC 213.

2 In view of the above, this Writ Petition is disposed of with liberty to avail of the statutory remedy.

3 If such revision is preferred within four weeks from today, the time spent by the Petitioner in this Court from 2.8.2018 till the passing of this order, would be considered as a ground for condonation of delay.
