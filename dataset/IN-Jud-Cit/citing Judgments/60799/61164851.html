JUDGMENT:

JUDGMENT 2005(13 )SCC 375 with Nos. 3533-95 of 1995, 5876-90 of 1994, 580 of 1995 and IA No. 3

1. During the course of hearing, Mr P.P. Rao, learned Senior Counsel for the appellants placed reliance on the decisions of this Court in State of Orissa v. Bhupendra Kumar Bose1962 Supp (2) SCR 380 : AIR 1962 SC 945 and T. Venkata Reddy v. State of A.P. (1985) 3 SCC 198 : 1985 SCC (L & S) 632 The learned Solicitor General brought to the notice of this Court the decision in State of Punjab v. Sat Pal Dang (1969) 1 SCR 478 : AIR 1969 SC

903. All these decisions are Constitution Bench decisions. Mr Rakesh Dwivedi, learned Senior Counsel for the respondent State of Bihar, however, relied on a nine-Judge Bench decision of this Court in S.R. Bommai v. Union of India (1994) 3 SCC 1 and in particular paras 283 to 290 thereof.

2. We are of the opinion that these matters call for hearing by a seven- Judge Bench of this Court. Be listed accordingly.
